Building up WordPress Website on GoDaddy

Wed, Jan 19, 2022 2-minute read

1. Go to GoDaddy, Choose your plan.

  • Get the free domain;
  • After selecting a free domain, Create an account in GoDaddy
  • Following the step to make a payment
  • Check the email to verify the email address, to use the domain just registered

2. Click Go to My Account

  • Under the Web Hosting, go to Set up, then adding Domain
  • Create WordPress account
  • Click Set Up My Site
  • Purchase an SSL certificate to secure the domain and make sure that it’s protected from hackers and other people trying to steal the information
  • Then go to the cPanel admin

3. Click Build Website, and Start Wizard

  • Setting up with few questions
  • Choose a Theme
  • Once theme is installed it will directly to the WordPress customizer
  • After X out of this, and having the WordPress website

4. Go to Setting

  • In the permalink settings, choose Post name

5. Head over to Plugins

  • Deleted all of the existing plugins
  • Adding elementor(website builder) plugin
  • Under the Appearance, choose the theme, you can add new theme, and active it
  • Choose Phlox as theme, and don’t forget to activating Phlox Core Plugin

6. Under the Phlox, find the Demo Importer

  • Deleted all of the existing plugins
  • Adding elementor(website builder) plugin
  • Under the Appearance, choose the theme, you can add new theme, and active it
  • Choose Phlox as theme, and don’t forget to activating Phlox Core Plugin
  • Under the demo importer, there are a lots of pre-built demo websites
  • Choose your favorite one, click on Importer
  • To check if they were successfully installed, go to Pages, then selecting all pages
  • Also check under Plugins

7. Edit all pages based on your preference.

  • Go back to Pages
  • Click all pages
  • Then under each page, click edit
  • Using the logomakr.com to design the logo
  • From Pexels.com get some great quality picture
